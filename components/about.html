About Me

Dr. Wong Seng Yue is an Associate Professor at Universiti Malaya, specializing in E-Learning, Game-Based Learning, and STEM education. With over 15 years of experience, he has led numerous research projects, published extensively, and received multiple awards for his contributions to academia.
